Aggieland Humane Society

Aggieland Humane Society is dedicated to helping cats and dogs permanent homes. Volunteers have the opportunity to spend the day taking care of these pets by feeding them, engaging in playtime, or going on walks. The experience is great because you get to give them affection until until they find other loving owners. I deeply value this organization because it allows you to form a bond with the animals, treating them as if they're your own, while contributing to their happiness and well-being. The sign up allows you to work with either cats or dogs, so they are open to both dog and cat lovers alike. The best part of the day is going in and finding out that another pet has been adopted.

Feed My Starving Children

Feed My Starving Children allows children around the world access to vital meals through donations and volunteer work. There are centers across the United States where volunteers crank music and diligently package boxes upon boxes of food. For those interested, it is an excellent experience especially with groups (as many hands makes the work go quick and keeps energy high). It is a highly fulfilling, fast paced way to spend a weekend with friends and family while making a difference. Time flew by incredibly quickly; the work was not monotonous, but rather a fun group experience battling global hunger.

Mission Arlington

Mission Arlington Mission Arlington is a non-profit organization by my home town. Volunteers play a crucial role bringing food, toys, clothing, and other needed items to be sorted and distributed to the community. After sorting, items are given to families in need. One of my favorite aspects of volunteering at Mission Arlington was handling books and toys to be given to the younger kids. Additionally, greeting donors who bring items in their cars was a excellent way to meet people in the community. As a more local organization, it felt more intimate and personal learning peoples' names. If you're in the area, I highly recommend taking the time to donate your time or extra possessions.
